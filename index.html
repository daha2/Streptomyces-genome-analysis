Comparative genomics and phylogenomic characterization of S. silvae

This project integrates laboratory microbiology and genome-based analysis to examine diversity, genomic structure, biosynthetic potential, and phenotypic traits within the S. silvae clade.

This website presents doctoral research on Streptomyces silvae, integrating DNA preparation, genome assembly, comparative genomics, phylogenomics, genome architecture analysis, biosynthetic gene cluster profiling, and physiology. The genomes of strains TMS4I1 and TMS12I2 were assembled from soil-derived isolates prepared in the laboratory. These strains were examined experimentally together with the type strain S. silvae For3T. Additional genomes included in the comparative analyses were obtained from public databases.

Deutsche Kurzfassung: Die Seite dokumentiert meine Promotionsarbeit in Mikrobiologie und Bioinformatik. Im Rahmen des Projekts habe ich DNA präpariert, Genome assembliert und die Stämme TMS4I1, TMS12I2 sowie den Typstamm For3T experimentell im Labor untersucht.
