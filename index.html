Comparative Genomics and Physiological Characterization of Streptomyces silvae

This project documents genomic and physiological analyses of Streptomyces silvae strains performed during doctoral research.

Project Sections

  • Comparative Genomics
  • Phylogenomics
  • Physiological Characterization of TMS4I1

Project Overview

The study investigates genomic diversity and ecological adaptability within the Streptomyces silvae clade using comparative genomics, phylogenomics, genome mining, and physiological characterization.
