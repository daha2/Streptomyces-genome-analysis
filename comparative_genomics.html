Comparative Genomics of Streptomyces silvae

This section summarizes the comparative genomic analysis of Streptomyces silvae strains, including newly sequenced soil isolates and publicly available reference genomes.

Dataset

  • Streptomyces silvae TMS4I1
  • Streptomyces silvae TMS12I2
  • Streptomyces silvae For3T
  • Streptomyces sp. M3
  • Streptomyces sp. gb1(2016)
  • Streptomyces sp. ADI93-02
  • Streptomyces sp. SID4921
  • Streptomyces sp. LamerLS-316

The dataset includes two newly sequenced isolates and selected publicly available genomes used for comparative analysis within the Streptomyces silvae clade.

Summary

Comparative genome analysis included genome assembly, genome quality assessment, annotation, ANI comparisons, genome synteny analysis, and genome mining of biosynthetic gene clusters.
